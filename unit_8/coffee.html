The Coffee Connection

lets you view up-to-date information, make reservations and access exclusive deals
- all in one easy app.

VIEW PROTOTYPE


CLIENT: Corner Coffee

SOFTWARE: Sketch, InVision


Corner Coffee is an independent coffee bar that takes pride in serving the best coffee and fresh food, sourced locally.


As the lead UX designer, I partnered with Corner Coffee to uncover their goals and design a user-centered application to meet the needs of their customers. My work on this project includes user research, information architecture, wireframes, and prototyping.


See my design documentation here.

USER RESEARCH

After interviewing Coffee Corner's customers and staff alike, I was able to narrow down the chief features of this application:



Real-time opening information: Management sometimes closes early leaving customers in the dark. With real-time hours on the Corner Coffee app, customers can save themselves the trip and management receives fewer customer complaints.


Deals and coupons: With plenty of coffee shops to choose from, loyal customers want perks. By downloading the app, customers have quick access to the latest deals and coupons which can easily be redeemed by just showing the coupon on their mobile device.


Book a table: No one wants to wait in line - let customers book ahead for a better coffee shop experience both online and in real life.


Event listings and search: Corner Coffee is not only a coffee shop but a community space for gatherings and events. By including events information in the app, customers may easily see what's happening at Coffee Corner in a few taps based on their unique interests.


Food and drinks menu: Let's face it: no one eats or drinks without looking up the menu first. For regular customers and new customers alike, the in-app menu keeps everyone up-to-date on Corner Coffee's tasty offerings and seasonal specialties.

USER FLOWS AND WIREFRAMES

With the user at the center of this app's design, I was able to take the chief features and create an interface that allows end-users to complete tasks with the least amount of clicks or taps possible.

HIGH FIDELITY PROTOTYPE

Having completed usability testing with wireframes, all user feedback was used to inform this high fidelity prototype. It features a clean, responsive design for all screens while staying true to Coffee Corner's well-known brand.
